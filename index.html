02
Adaptive Intelligence

Motion-Adaptive
Boost Mode

A technical tour de force.

Scaling adaptive lighting to street-level requires exceptional engineering expertise and precision. PIR sensor detects movement within 8m radius. Luminaire boosts to 100% output on detection and returns to 30% energy-saving idle after 90 seconds. Up to 40% additional energy saving per night.

PIR · 8m radius · Patented system
03
Energy Storage

LiFePO4
Battery System

5–10 Year Operational Life

The case embodies a seamless fusion of energy density and safety. LiFePO4 chemistry is thermally stable to 270°C — no risk of thermal runaway. 2,000+ full charge cycles. Integrated Battery Management System prevents over-charge, over-discharge and cell imbalance.

IP67 sealed · BMS protection

Genesis

Half A Decade
In The Making

From solar yield simulations to field stress tests, we meticulously engineered SRB-01, ensuring energy autonomy, structural integrity, and urban-grade reliability.

2018

The obsession begins. A vision for off-grid street lighting that doesn't look like an afterthought — driven by design precision and engineering rigour.

2019

Inspired by automotive aerodynamics, aerospace weight philosophy, and the precision of precision instruments. First sketches of the integrated pole-panel system.

2020

From concept to prototype. Three principles crystallised: autonomy, intelligence, longevity. The LiFePO₄ chemistry selected after 18 months of comparative testing.

2021

Pioneering adaptive dimming with PIR precision. Patent filed for motion-boost algorithm. Prototypes #01 and #02 deployed in Kochi and Thiruvananthapuram field trials.

2022

Luminaire housing developed with automotive die-cast partners. IoT module certified to 3GPP R13. BMS controller achieves target: 2,000+ cycle life.

2023

1,200 hours of continuous operational testing. IP65 certification achieved. MNRE type-approval obtained. Smart City Mission pilot approved: 500 units, Kerala.

2025

SRB-01 debut. Commercial production commences. Available in three configurations. Exclusively by project consultation to allocated municipal and institutional partners.
